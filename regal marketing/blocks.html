Blocks

We manufacture concrete block of highest quality. Concrete blocks, also commonly known as ‘Building Blocks’ are composed entirely of concrete. These blocks are made up of cement, aggregate and water. All block types are manufactured using high grade raw material, ensuring their durability. We offer hollow as well as solid blocks in different dimensions and designs, according to the demands of our clients.

Solid Blocks:

Solid blocks are one of the types of concrete blocks, coming in different dimensions and shapes. Due to their fine quality, life span and versatile nature, solid blocks are often preferred over bricks. These blocks are strong and provide good standability for structures. For load bearing walls, solid bricks are the preferred option. They are available in larger sizes as compared to bricks. Though they can sometimes be perceived as an expensive choice over red brick, their rounded benefits make them a worthwhile investment.

Features:

  • Speed up construction process, due to their larger size

  • Compressive strength of 1500 PSI, can be increased by demand,

  • Resistant to abrasion is weather

  • Lighter in weight as compared to clay bricks

  • Save up on construction costs

  • Low maintenance costs due to their durability/p>

  • Fire Resistant

  • These blocks have good heat and sounds insulation

  • Durable, strong and Versatile, can last up to 30 years

Applicable Uses:

  • Exterior walls.

  • Partition walls.

  • Interior walls.

  • Boundary walls.

Available Sizes:

  • 6”x8”x12

  • 4”x8”x12

  • 8”x8”x12

Hollow Blocks:

We manufacture high quality hollow blocks, highly in demand in various construction projects. Unlike solid blocks, these blocks have a hollow void in the inner area, that takes up to 25% of the gross area of the block. These blocks are heavier than solid blocks, often used as partition walls in multi-storey buildings. Because of their hollow nature, they add to the heat and sound insulation along with reducing weight of the structure. We offer these blocks in different dimensions and designs suiting best with the demand of our clients.

Features:

  • Suitable for front elevation of a building, partitioned wall, landscaping, or for boundary walls.

  • Economical, durable, reusable.

  • Speed up the construction process.

  • Resistant to fire and extreme weather conditions

  • Easy to install and take up less space

  • Lighter in weight and size as compared to clay bricks.

Applicable Uses:

  • Exterior walls.

  • Partition walls.

  • Interior walls.

  • Boundary walls.

Available Sizes:

  • 6”x8”x16
