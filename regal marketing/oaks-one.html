Oaks One

If you are looking for comfort, affordability and a lavish location, Oaks one might be your next stop. Located at a prestigious location from the main city, Oaks one brings you the lush scenic landscape views of Botanical Gardens and Marilla Hills. The project is set in the commercial grounds of Park View City, giving it both commercial as well as residential value in market. The community has been tailored thoroughly to provide all modern amenities and facilities for its residents and investors. The development and location have been carefully set to ensure guaranteed profit to investors. If you are looking for residence in the heart of Islamabad, or to invest for future projects, Regal Marketing proudly takes you under confidence with this scheme.

Location of Regal Marketing is one of the key features that makes the community stand out, claiming its market value. Located in Park view, in Zone IV of Islamabad, the project is a short 15 minutes’ drive from main city. The site is linked to Banni Gala and Chak Shazad Farm Houses, adding to its scenic beauty. Surrounded by the Botanical Gardens and Margalla Hills, the community gives an endless vista of lush greens to its residents.

Oaks One, NOC:

The No Objection Certificate (NOC) of Oaks one is approved by the Capital Development Authority (CDA). With NOC, Oaks one has set its mark as one of the leading projects in Islamabad, making it a more secure and profitable site for investment and residence.

Facilities:

Oaks one is bringing new found innovation and creativity to those looking for a prestigious location in Islamabad, offering comfort, facilities and style, all in one. This commercial center is driving in more work-force, making it an ideal setting for your offices and work. The community is providing all residential facilities you can look for in this era of comfort and innovation. Luxurious apartments combined with the lavishing landscapes, gives you the opportunity to express your individuality. Some of its prominent amenities include:

Smart Apartments:

Oaks one brings you apartments and studios tailored with all the basic and lavish facilities you can find in a modern community. The apartments are installed with luxurious facilities and sensor light technology, adding to its grand touch. Apartment Balconies opening to scenic landscapes gives the society an eco-friendly look while offering the residents a spectacular view. The apartments have Smart doors and Smart locks installed, giving a more secure solution then traditional deadbolt. The smart locks can be accessed through remotes, providing more comfort and convenience.

Lake Centered Society:

Located in the heart of Park View City, a 100 kanal artificial lake is being constructed. The site gives a spectacular view to the society adding to its scenic beauty and eco-friendly look.

Main Boulevard:

Oaks one stands in Main boulevard, which is one of the most iconic and prominent structures in Park View City, Islamabad.

Video Doorbells:

Oaks one apartments are installed with video doorbell, allowing you to view the person standing on the other side of door without opening it. This provides two-way communication without having to interact face to face with the party on other side.

Master Plans:

Oaks one comprises of world class amenities with a master plan. The community has been tailored and designed to attract high demand enterprises, commercial zones and a safe community for residents. Oaks one is giving its investors an edge of security with NOC.

The project comprises of 6 floors, their architecture and planning has been set as following:

Shops:

The ground floor has been set for commercial shops. According to the needs and requirements of investors, 9 shops comprising of different sizes have been set on ground floor.

Offices:

The 1st and 2nd floor has been set with well structured offices. The offices have been designed to be more employee-centric, with modern facilities, giving them a more comfortable touch for employee interaction and growth. New approaches to office customization are making Oaks one the ideal setting for offices.

Apartments:

The 3rd, 4th ,5th and 6th floors are designed and planned to have 2- and 3-bedroom apartments and studios. With modern layouts, smart housing, luxurious facilities and furnishing, the apartments are providing the ideal residence for you.

One-bedroom Apartments/studios:

Bringing one bed room apartment and studios, Oaks one is providing you with the solution of compact living that is set to be a global trend in near future.
