New Metro City Gujar Khan is a state of art development launched by the BSM group that is directly associated with Bahria Town as the project is owned by the grandson of Malik Riaz, Malik Bilal. The housing plan is a solely launched TMA-approved development project, occupying a prime location adjacent to GT Road. New Metro City Gujar Khan Land

After the success of New Metro City Kharian and Gwadar Golf City, the BSM Developers have decided to deliver their real estate services in Gujar Khan, which is the form of New Metro City Kharian. The housing scheme is approved by the Tehsil Municipal Administration (TMA) and extends over 50,000 Kanal of land. Residential and commercial plots ranging from 5 Marla to 20 Marla are available for possession within the housing scheme with limited inventory in the market.

File Receiving Process

The process of file retaliating is as follows:

  • Regal Marketing receives Files from the New Metro City Housing society Gujar Khan.

  • After receiving, files are thoroughly filled as per the credentials provided to us by our worthy customers.

  • After the file filling process, files will be sent to the Housing society for receiving process.

  • After the receiving process, the file will be stamped and sent back to Regal Marketing.

  • Files will be delivered to the members who have paid the down payment and processing fee.

  • If you have paid your down payment and process fee, then you will receive your files.

Early Bird Balloting FAQs

As the Early Bird Balloting is approaching fast and for the convenience of their investors, New Metro City policies have announced some Frequently Announced Questions. Following are the Early Bird Balloting FAQs notified by the New Metro City Gujar Khan Officials:

What are the policies of Early Bird Balloting?

Early Bird Policy means that the customers who will file and submit their documents with down payment before 19th August shall be allotted plots in the A block sector.

What are the dates of Early Bird Balloting?

As per the policies of the New Metro City Gujar Khan, all the clients should submit their forms by 19th August 2022 to become a part of Early Bird Balloting.

Will the file holders who submitted their files before 20th August 2022 get a plot in Block A?

The customer who has submitted their files before 19th august will become a part of the New Metro City Early Bird balloting for Block.

Is there any confirmation fee required for the Block A plots?

No confirmation fee is required until the plot is officially confirmed in Block A.

Will the Plot Number be allocated in Block A Blotting?

The allotted files in Early Balloting Block A date on 20th August 2022 plot number will be announced soon after the ceremony of Early Bird Balloting.

If a person fails if a plot is not allotted to the file holder in Block A, where would he be allotted the plot?

All the forms issued in the Early Balloting will be allotted plots in Block of New Metro City Gujar Khan. Those who are not assigned plots in Block A will be assigned plots in the other blocks of the residential unit.
